# Governance Is the Knowledge Home

Date: 2026-04-09

## Decision
Tracked knowledge artifacts live in `hldpro-governance`; governed repos keep only pointers and local runtime helpers.

## Why
- Prevents graph/wiki duplication across repos
- Keeps weekly sweep write-back in one auditable place
- Preserves product repos as product codebases, not memory stores
